Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam

time line of A.P.J Abdul Kalam

  • 1931 - Born on 15 October to a Tamil family in Rameswaram
  • 1954 - Graduated in physics in 1954 from Madras University
  • 1955 - moved to Madras to study aerospace engineering in Madras Institute of Technology
  • 1960 - Joined Defence Research and Development Organisation(DRDO)
  • 1969 - Transferred to Indian Research Organisation(ISRO) as project director
  • 1980 - Sucessfully deployed the Rohini satellight in near-earth orbit
  • 1970-99 - Project Devil,Project Valiant,Agni,Prithvi,Chief Scientific Advisor to Prime Minister and the Secretary of Defence Research and Development Organisation
  • 2002-2007 - Served as President of India
  • 2015 - We lost him forever
